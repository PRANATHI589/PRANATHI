The Leave Management System is a Web based application that can be used over the intranet
It standardizes a common universal process for entire Organization
It uses the standard leave approval process of Initiating Officer and reviewing officer.
Generates required Reports for the Organization
Intimation of Approved/Rejected Leaves on E-mail.
The main uses and benefits of such an easy leave System are:
Effectively address the leave management requirements of your organization, be it small or big manage your employees’ leaves with efficiency, transparency and accuracy
Define work-week and holiday calendars
Improve employee morale, influence employee behavior
Define leave types (Vacation, Sick leave, Unpaid leave, or another custom absence type)
Define employee reporting structures / approval hierarchy
Set up leave accrual and carry forward rules
View summary reports for all employees
Allow the manager to efficiently manage leave approvals and allowances per employeev Minimize unauthorized leave
Provide reports for improved analysis and decision making regarding staffing and workload
Enables quick integration with employee calendar of choice: Google/Outlook/Apple
Facilitates intuitive self-service functionality to all staff
Ensure accuracy and reduce compliance risk
Provides key data updated in real time for Payroll and makes sure that HR avoids overpaying for earned leave time
